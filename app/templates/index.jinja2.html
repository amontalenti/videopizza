The problem: In today’s world of short attention spans and wide range of device platforms, how can we entice people to watch long-form video content on the web?

The solution: VideoPizza is a navigation app for viewers and a narrative presentation tool for creators. It allows users to "trailer-ize" their work or to consume a video on their own terms.

For example, perhaps you have a boring C-SPAN video you want to slice?
